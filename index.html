The Developer

I can tell you that I've been involved professionally in web develpment for over 10 years, but technology and ideas change so rappidly -- Whats been popular 10 years ago has little significance today. My philosophy is simple: develop software for end-users, utilize modern technology, build on experience and don't let the past dictate the future. But, i think I'm getting ahead of myself...

The Technology

... there is a difference between what's in vogue and what's practical. Technology is subjective. As a developer, my goal is to be stack-agnostic as much as possible. My current preferance is Javascript, in particular Node.js, Angular.js, Express.js, but I've developed on ASP.NET C# stack, PHP and Laravel, MySQL, MSSQL, Mongodb as well jQuery. Indeed, lets not not forget the essentials HTML5 and CSS3.
